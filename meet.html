DR. ROBERT J SILONGeneral Dentistry and AEGD

  • B.A., Cornell University
  • DDS, New York University
  • Clinical Director for the General
    Practitioner Residency from 1980-1984
  • Member of the ADA, NYSDA and NCDS.

Dr. Silon has been a staple of the Rockville Centre community for almost 30 years. Dr. Silon‘s gentle and caring approach has been celebrated by all of his patients. His commitment to futhering dentistry has lead him down a humanitarian path where he is actively involved with medical and dental missions to Africa. Dr. Silon has donated his time and dental skill in treating the impoverished villagers in Uganda. This has become a labor of love for him and something he plans on doing for many more years.
