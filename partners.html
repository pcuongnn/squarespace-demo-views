What We Look For

We are currently seeking to affiliate with practices in the tri-state region (NY, NJ, CT) with the following characteristics:

  • Providers who are interested in building a relationship with the Smilist
  • Well-established, insurance/private-pay patient base
  • Staff interested in remaining employed with our company post transaction but we are open to situations where providers are seeking to transition and/or reduce their workload
  • No minimum/maximum annual revenue requirement
